Administrator Responsibilities

  • Job Approval: Review all job postings for appropriateness, accuracy, and compliance with campus policies.
  • Quality Control: Ensure job descriptions are clear, professional, and provide accurate information.
  • User Oversight: Monitor user accounts and deactivate accounts if necessary to maintain system integrity.
  • System Maintenance: Regularly check for pending approvals and respond promptly to keep the system running smoothly.

Job Approval Guidelines

Approve If:

  • Job description is clear and detailed
  • Position is appropriate for students
  • Salary/wages are reasonable
  • Contact information is provided
  • Posting follows campus policies

Reject If:

  • Description is vague or incomplete
  • Position seems inappropriate or unsafe
  • Contains discriminatory language
  • Appears to be spam or fraud
  • Violates campus employment policies
