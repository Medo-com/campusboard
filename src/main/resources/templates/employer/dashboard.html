Employer Quick Guide

  • Create Detailed Postings: Include clear job descriptions, requirements, and expectations to attract the right candidates.
  • Admin Approval Required: All job postings must be approved by an administrator before students can see them.
  • Review Applications Promptly: Check your job postings regularly to see student applications.
  • Update as Needed: You can edit your postings at any time. Updates will need re-approval.

Understanding Job Status

PENDING

Your job is awaiting admin review. Students cannot see it yet.

APPROVED

Your job is live! Students can view and apply to this position.

REJECTED

Your job was not approved. You can edit and resubmit it.
